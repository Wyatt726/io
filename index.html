About me!

My name is Wyatt Orman, I am 26 years old and was born and raised in the greater Oakland town of Antioch, CA.
I am currently living in Hubert, NC, with my wife Shelby, and two dogs Chewy and Sadie.
When I was 19, I joined the USMC and served until a week before my 26th birthday.
During my time in the service, I spent five years stationed in 29 Palms, CA where I
was a forward observer, company level intelligence cell leader, as well as machine gun
section assistant leader for the "lucky" rifle company, weapons section of 3RD BN 4TH Marines.
During my time in "lucky" company, I deployed to Australia for a year, where I trained the
ADF as well as French Paratroopers. When I was 22, a year after returning from Australia, I was
headed to Afghanistan. I spent 9 months total in Helamnd Province and 7 of that was in the heart
of the notorious Sangin Valley District. Once I returned home as a 3x Navy and Marine Corp Achievement
Medal with combat Valor and Combat Action Ribbon recipient , I received a special duty assignement to serve in USCYBERCOM
under the NSA. This is where I began my journey in the world of cyber. I spent my first year learning the
new skills I would have to behold in order to efficentatly do my job. I served as an All Source Intelligence
Analyst as well as offensive cyber operations targeteer. After two years in the NSA, I had decieded my time in
the military was ready for a close, which is when I moved to NC and began my software developer
journey with devCodeCamp.
